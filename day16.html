ROSE CATEGORIES

OLD GARDEN ROSE FLOWER

Often referred to as “antique” roses and “historic” roses, the Old Garden Rose has been around since before 1867.

WHILD ROSE FLOWER

Considered the wildflower type of rose, Wild Roses, or “species roses,” lack the cross-breeding history and hybridization of other modern varieties.

MODERN GARDEN ROSE FLOWERS

Where Old Garden Roses bloom once per year, Modern Roses offer a continuous bloom, as well as a larger bloom size.
